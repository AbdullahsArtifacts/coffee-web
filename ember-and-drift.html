Our Practice

Three people. One bar. No machine louder than the conversation.

Ember & Drift opened in 2021 with one rule: nothing gets served faster than it should. We buy green beans direct from three small farms, roast in batches of nine kilos, and brew everything by hand — pour-over, immersion, or cold drip, never push-button espresso.

Most cafés optimize for throughput. We optimize for the four minutes it takes a V60 to bloom, drain, and reward you for waiting.

The Pour

How a V60 actually gets made here

00:00

Bloom

40g of water at 94°C wakes the grounds — we watch for the rise, not the clock.

00:30

First pour

A slow spiral from center to edge, doubling the bed weight in water.

01:45

Second pour

Topping off in stages keeps the bleed steady and the extraction even.

03:30

Drawdown

The bed flattens, the last drips clear, and the cup is ready to pour.
